# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy
community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Give and accept technical feedback based on evidence.
- Take responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes happen.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community.

Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, personal attacks, sexualized attention, deliberate disruption, and
publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate
this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they consider inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Report conduct concerns privately to berke@kwilabs.com. Maintainers will respect the reporter's privacy and respond
in a manner appropriate to the circumstances. Enforcement decisions may range from a private correction through a
temporary or permanent ban.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces and when someone is officially representing the project in public spaces.

This policy is based on the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
